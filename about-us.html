About Us

Canada Citizenship Help is a branch of Canada AG Immigration & Citizenship Services. Canada AG Immigration & Citizenship Services is a Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant (RCIC), a registered and licensed member of the College of Immigration and Citizenship Consultants (CICC) in good standing, as well as a proud member of the Canadian Association of Professional Immigration Consultants (CAPIC) and an accredited member of Better Business Bureau (BBB).

We will assist you with filling out the proper forms, helping to identify the appropriate supporting documents, finding a qualified photographer that specializes in Canadian citizenship photos, and assessing your application, documents, and photos prior to submission. We will submit your application to the government and represent you during the entire process until you receive your Canadian citizenship certificate. Our services include:

  • Assess individual circumstances for eligibility for Canadian citizenship and advise on the best entry approach
  • Prepare and submit applications and supporting documentation on behalf of our clients
  • Act as a liaison between our clients and government agencies, such as the Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC)

Our goal is to create a positive experience for our clients during such an important decision. We are not only here to help you find the right way to obtain your Canadian Citizenship certificate, but also to make the entire process easier, faster, and more efficient. Canada Citizenship Help offers unique innovative online services to help you access our services 24/7.

With our professional guidance and innovative technology, you will be a few steps closer to your Canadian Citizenship Dream.
